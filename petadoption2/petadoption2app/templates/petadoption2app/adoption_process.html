Adoption Process

Eligibility Requirements

Before adopting a pet, please ensure you meet the following requirements:

  • Must be at least 21 years old.
  • Have a valid government-issued ID.
  • Provide proof of current residence (rental agreement or mortgage statement).
  • Consent from all household members for the adoption.
  • Ability to provide a stable and loving home environment.

Application Process

Follow these steps to adopt a pet:

  1. Browse Available Pets: Visit our Available Pets page to view pets currently up for adoption.
  2. Fill Out an Application: Complete our online Adoption Application form with your details.
  3. Initial Review: Our team will review your application and may contact you for additional information.
  4. Meet and Greet: If your application is approved, we will schedule a meet-and-greet session with the pet.
  5. Home Visit: A home visit may be conducted to ensure a safe and suitable environment for the pet.
  6. Finalize Adoption: Once all steps are completed, you can finalize the adoption by signing the adoption agreement and paying the adoption fee.

Adoption Fees

Our adoption fees help cover the costs of veterinary care, food, and shelter for the pets. The fees are as follows:

  • Dogs: $150 - $300
  • Cats: $75 - $150
  • Small Animals: $25 - $75
  • Senior Pets (7+ years): $50

All adopted pets come vaccinated, microchipped, and spayed/neutered.

Post-Adoption Support

We offer post-adoption support to ensure a smooth transition for both you and your new pet. Our services include:

  • Behavioral advice and training resources.
  • Health and wellness tips.
  • Access to our veterinary partners for ongoing care.
  • 24/7 helpline for any urgent concerns.
